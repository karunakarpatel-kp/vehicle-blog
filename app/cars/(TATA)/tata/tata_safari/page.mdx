The New Safari comes with Advanced ESP features. For those who do not know what it is, the Electronic Stability Program (ESP) is a safety technology that combines an anti-lock braking system (ABS) and a traction control system (TCS).

This technology helps in detecting and reducing loss of traction which prevents tires from skidding, reduces engine power, breaks the inside rear wheel to help steer the car, and many more. The new safari is equipped with all the ESP features.
